Welcome

The Beans Family makes original animated songs for kids. Every song starts with a real idea, gets shaped by a human and an AI working together, and ends up as a tune you will catch yourself humming.

Pull up a chair and stay a while.

How It Works

Every Beans song goes through the same four steps:

  1. Human + AI co-write. A person and an AI brainstorm a song together: the idea, the lyrics, the feel.
  2. Suno generations. The song goes into Suno. Many, many generations are made until one hits home perfectly.
  3. Animated in Blender. The chosen song gets dropped into something like Blender and animated out scene by scene.
  4. Final human edit. A person does a final editing pass to make sure every beat hits just right.
