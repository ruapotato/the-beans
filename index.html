Curious how a song goes from a seed of an idea to a finished video? Check out the How It Works tab.

How It Works

Every song we release goes through the same loving process:

1. Human + AI write the song together. A person and an AI collaborate on the lyrics and musical direction, trading ideas until the song has a heart.
2. Many generations in Suno. The song is generated in Suno, over and over. We keep iterating until one of the generations hits home perfectly. No shortcuts.
3. Animation in Blender. The Suno song gets dropped into a tool like Blender and gets animated to match the music.
4. Final human editing pass. A human does one more pass to make sure every beat hits just right, every cut lands, and the whole thing feels alive.
