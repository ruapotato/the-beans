How It Works

Every song starts with an idea and ends with a beat that hits just right. Here is how we get there:

  1. Step 1

    Human + AI brainstorm the song

    A human and an AI collaborate on the lyrics, melody, and story. Ideas bounce back and forth until we have something we love.
  2. Step 2

    Into Suno it goes

    We run the song through Suno and generate many, many versions. We keep listening until one hits home perfectly.
  3. Step 3

    Animated in Blender

    That final Suno song gets dropped into something like Blender, where the characters come to life frame by frame.
  4. Step 4

    Final editing pass

    A human does the final edit to make sure every beat, every cut, and every little moment lands exactly right.
